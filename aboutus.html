About us

Here’s Our Story

Balance Buddy is a leading mental health organization in Sri Lanka, dedicated to supporting emotional and psychological well-being through compassionate and accessible services. As pioneers in online counseling within the country, we offer secure and confidential therapy via messaging, voice, and video sessions—empowering clients to seek help comfortably from their homes.

Our clinic is backed by a team of certified and experienced mental health professionals who specialize in a wide range of therapeutic approaches. With decades of combined expertise, our counselors are equipped to help individuals navigate stress, anxiety, trauma, relationship issues, and other personal challenges. We are committed to creating a safe space where people of all backgrounds feel heard, understood, and supported on their healing journey.

Beyond individual care, we also provide tailored solutions for organizations and institutions, including psychological assessments, mental wellness training, and workplace counseling programs. Our mission is to enhance mental health awareness, foster emotional resilience, and contribute to building a healthier, more compassionate community—one conversation at a time.
